典藏捐贈展以新增資料夾為題‍
體現藝博館典藏品身份的認定過程
經過一系列的程序
編列物件／作品清單、整理檢視、增設圖庫
透過典藏委員會進行專業審核或鑑定
最後正式「建檔」成為藝博館典藏品
電腦螢幕中 游標點下「新增資料夾」的動作
成為典藏品登錄作業的關鍵
也揭示典藏研究工作背後的步驟與細節
新增資料夾意味著「開啟」典藏品的世界

‍

COLLECTION HIGHLIGHTS 2022 presents the identificationprocess of the Our Museum’s collections with the title of NEWFOLDERS. This series of procedures included cataloging,inventory, adding an image gallery, furthermore conductingprofessional review or appraisal through the collectionscommittee, and finally officially “filing” into the Our Museum’scollections. To Click “Create a New Folder” on the computerscreen with the cursor becomes the key to the documentationof collections, which also reveals the steps and details behindthe collection research work. Creating a new folder means“opening” the world of collections.
